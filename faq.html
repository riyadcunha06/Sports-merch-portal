Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website about? +
This is an eCommerce platform offering sports merchandise from top brands like Nike, Puma, Adidas, and Reebok. We provide products for Men, Women, and Kids, along with essential sports accessories.
Do I need to create an account to place an order? +
Yes, users must sign up and log in to place an order for security and a better shopping experience.
How does the 'Add to Cart' button work? +
The 'Add to Cart' button only activates after selecting a size to ensure valid selection before checkout.
Can I update or remove items from my cart? +
Yes, users can modify the quantity of items or remove products from their cart before proceeding to checkout.
How is the order summary displayed? +
After a successful purchase, the Thank You page dynamically displays the order details, including the items purchased and total amount.
What payment methods are supported? +
We support debit cards, UPI, and net banking for seamless transactions.
Is there a guest checkout option? +
No, users must log in to ensure a smooth and secure shopping experience.
How will I know if my payment was successful? +
After a successful payment, you will receive an email confirmation with your order details, including the payment summary and expected delivery date.
