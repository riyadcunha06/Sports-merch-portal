SHOE CARE GUIDE

Keep your kicks looking fresh with our expert care tips

Why Proper Shoe Care Matters

Proper shoe care not only extends the life of your favorite footwear but also maintains their performance and appearance. Follow our comprehensive guide to keep your shoes looking and feeling their best.

Care By Material

Leather Care

  • Remove surface dirt with a soft brush or damp cloth
  • Clean with a mild soap solution and soft cloth
  • Apply leather conditioner to prevent drying and cracking
  • Use a leather protector spray for water resistance
  • Polish occasionally to maintain appearance
  • Allow to dry naturally away from direct heat

Palmero Care

  • Clean with a soft, dry cloth to remove surface dust
  • For light stains, use a specialized Palmero leather cleaner
  • Apply Palmero conditioning cream every 2-3 months
  • Avoid exposure to direct sunlight for extended periods
  • Use shoe trees when not wearing to maintain shape
  • Store in a breathable dust bag in a cool, dry place

Canvas & Mesh Care

  • Remove loose dirt with a soft brush
  • Hand wash with mild soap and lukewarm water
  • Use a soft brush to gently scrub stained areas
  • Rinse thoroughly to remove soap residue
  • Air dry completely before wearing
  • Apply fabric protector to prevent future stains

Synthetic Material Care

  • Wipe with a damp cloth to remove surface dirt
  • Clean with mild soap solution and soft cloth
  • Rinse cloth thoroughly and wipe away soap residue
  • Use specialized synthetic cleaner for tough stains
  • Air dry at room temperature
  • Apply synthetic protector spray periodically

Step-by-Step Cleaning Guide

1

Prepare Your Shoes

Remove laces and insoles. Brush off loose dirt and debris.

2

Clean the Upper

Use appropriate cleaning method based on material (see material guides above).

3

Clean Soles

Scrub outsoles with a brush and mild soap solution to remove dirt and grime.

4

Dry Properly

Allow shoes to air dry at room temperature. Avoid direct heat or sunlight.

5

Apply Protection

Once dry, apply appropriate protector spray based on material type.

Regular Maintenance Tips

Rotate Your Shoes

Alternate between different pairs to allow shoes to fully dry and recover their shape between wears.

Air Them Out

After wearing, allow your shoes to breathe before storing them to prevent odor and bacteria buildup.

Protect Regularly

Reapply protector sprays every few weeks or after cleaning to maintain water and stain resistance.

Proper Storage

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Use shoe trees for premium footwear.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my shoes?

It depends on how frequently you wear them and the conditions they're exposed to. As a general rule, light cleaning after every few wears and deep cleaning once a month for regularly worn shoes is recommended.

Can I machine wash my shoes?

We don't recommend machine washing as it can damage the structure and materials of your shoes. Hand cleaning using appropriate methods for each material is always preferable.

How do I remove bad odors from my shoes?

To eliminate odors, sprinkle baking soda inside your shoes and let it sit overnight. You can also use specialized shoe deodorizers or fabric fresheners. Ensuring shoes dry completely between wears helps prevent odor buildup.

How can I protect white shoes from yellowing?

Store white shoes away from direct sunlight, as UV rays can cause yellowing. Apply a UV-protective spray, clean them regularly, and use white shoe polish or specialized whitening products to maintain their brightness.
